Den Ngoc Son Temple

  • Vietnam

This is an early morning view of the Huc bridge that leads to the Den Ngoc Son Temple on the edge of Hoan Kiem Lake in central Hanoi, Vietnam. This bridge is known as the "place where morning sunlight rests" as can be seen in this image. The temple was founded in the 14th century, but the buildings currently on the island date to the 18th century.

Young Woman At Bridge

  • Myanmar

This 17 year-old young woman has been selling inexpensive jewelry at the famous U Bein bridge in Amarpuna for five years. Her English is impeccable. I spent a while with her trying to get a good portrait of her with an innocuous background. This was the best shot of the group. I wondered if she was still in school and what future she saw for herself. with her excellent English and good looks. I should have asked her these questions, but I only thought about them later. Olympus OMD EM-5, 33mm, 1/160@f4.5.
