Bearded man, yellow robe

  • India

I would pin my red cloth against barred windows on the street and ask people to pose for me. I would take two photos each, and at times had a line of four or five people waiting to have their photo taken. I gave them no directions how to stand, and each subject just took a few seconds to photograph. This image was taken during the Kumbh Mela festival in Haridwar, Uttarakhand. Nikon D700, 62mm, 1/500@f 5.
