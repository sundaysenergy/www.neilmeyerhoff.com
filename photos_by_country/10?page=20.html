Ruwanwelisya Dajoba

  • Sri Lanka

Ruwanwelisiya Dagoba, 2nd c. BCE, 55 meters tall, known for frieze of elephants around the base, at Anuradhapura which is a UNESCO site. Begun by King Dutugemuna, and restorations over the years have changed the original shape, thought to be bubble shaped. Olympus OMD EM-1, 1/400@f13, 12mm, ISO 200

Seal Colony, Mowe Bay

  • Namibia
