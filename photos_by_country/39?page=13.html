INDIAN WOMAN IN CHURCH, LA PARROQUIA

  • Mexico

On the first Friday of March, Indians from the surrounding area come to San Miguel to celebrate the Feast of the Lord of the Conquest. They wear these elaborate home made costumes and dance in the streets after praying in the La Parroquia church. This vertical panoramic photograph was taken with a Hasselblad X-pan camera using a 45mm lens.
