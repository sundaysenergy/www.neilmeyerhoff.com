Convento de El Carmen Alto

  • Ecuador

The Convento de El Carmen Alto belongs to a an order of cloistered Carmelite nuns and was constructed in the 18th century. The convent lies within the UNESCO World Heritage site in Quito, Ecuador which was designates a heritage site due to the completeness of the Spanish colonial city that remains. This photograph was taken with a Hasselblad HD-31 camera.

Click image to enlarge.

Seafront of Cavtat

  • Croatia

Cavtat is a pretty port village on the Dalmatian coast south of Dubrovnik with many Italian influences. Italy had control of this southern Croatian town until early in the 20th century and was attacked by the Serbs during the wars of the 1990s. This photograph was taken with a Hasselblad HD-31 camera.

Young Girl At Shwedagon Pagoda

  • Myanmar

On our last day in Myanmar, we again visited the Shwedagon Pagoda. I saw this girl sitting with her brother and mother at the edge of one of the small shrines. Rather than walk right up to her and try and take her photograph, I sat down next to her and for a few moments did not pay any attention to her. Then I looked at her, we made eye contact, and then I raised the camera and took a few photos of her. Olympus OMD EM-5, 24mm, 1/250@f32.
