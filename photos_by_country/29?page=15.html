Reclining Buddha Statue, Dambulla Cave Temples

  • Sri Lanka

Reclining Buddha statue in Cave III of Dambulla Cave Temples, 1st century BCE, UNESCO site. King Valagambahu took refuge in these caves after being exiled from Anuradhapura, and turned the caves into temples as thanks when he regained the throne. The wall and ceiling paintings date from the 2nd to 18th centuries. Olympus OMD,EM-1, 2.0 secs, F9.0, 10mm, ISO 400
