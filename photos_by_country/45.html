OLD NUN

  • Bhutan

As we were driving from Thimphu to Paro, Bhutan, we saw this older nun standing next to a small grocery stand by the side of the road. We stopped and asked if we could take her picture, to which she agreed. This vertical panoramic photograph was taken with a Hasselblad X-pan camera.

VERMILLION TORII, FUSHIMI-INARI SHRINE

  • Japan

The Fushimi-Inari Shinto shrine in the southern part of the city of Kyoto, Japan, is know for these torii that line the hillside above the main temple. Each of these arches honors or memorializes someone. The Japanese characters are carved on the other side of the torii shown here. This panoramic photograph was taken with a Hasselblad X-pan camera.

Tomb of Unknown Soldier

  • Greece

Military officer saluting the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier at the Plateia Syntagma in central Athens, Greece. The building to the rear is the National Parliament, built in 1842 as a palace for King Otto. In 1930 a figure of a dying Greek soldier was carved into the wall to create the Unknown Soldier memorial to all Greek soldiers who have died since independence. This photograph was made with a Hasselblad HD-31 digital camera.

Plaza Grande

  • Ecuador

This is an early morning view of Plaza Grande, with the Presidential office building in the rear. The historic area of Quito, Ecuador is an UNESCO World Heritage Site, it being one of the most intact Spanish colonial cities in the Americas. This photograph was taken with a Hasselblad HD-31 camera.

  • Croatia

Baska Voda is a small port city south of Split where many sailboats are for hire to sail along the Dalmatian coast of Croatia. The portion of the coast is often called the Mararska Riviera. This photograph was taken with a Hasselblad HD-31 camera.
