Flavored Water Salesman

  • Morocco

Flavored water salesman in souk, Fès, Morocco. These flavored waters are made by women in their homes by boiling water through flowered petals in the devices at top of picture. The women bring the bottles of water to the salesman, and he gives back to the women a percentage of the sale price when they are sold. Olympus OMD-EM-1, ISO 500, 14mm, 1/30@f5.6
