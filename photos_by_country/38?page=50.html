Monks Lined Up To Enter Dining Hall

  • Myanmar

The monks at the Mahagandhayon Monastery line up every morning for their first meal of the day at 10:30AM. Many tourists also line up looking for the best perspective from which to photograph the monks. Olympus OMD EM-5, 35mm, 1/160@f2.8.

Click image to enlarge.

El Badi Palace

  • Morocco

El Badi Palace, built by Ahmed el Mansour in the late 16th century, was mostly razed after his death, and was modeled on the Alhambra in Spain. This nine shot, hand held, panoramic was stitched together in Photoshop CC. Olympus OMD EM-1, ISO 200, 12mm, 1/100@f10.

Click image to enlarge.

Buddha Statue at Gangaramaya Temple

  • Sri Lanka

The Gangaramaya Temple, founded in the mid-19th century, is often a place of prayer for important political leaders and business men, so the temple has many offerings on display. This large Buddha statue, in the meditation pose, is housed in the main image house of the temple. Olympus OMD EM-1, 1/20@f3.5, 12mm, ISO 200

The Riva

  • Croatia

Trogir is a small medieval city in the central Dalmatian coast of Croatia. The town is on a tiny island connected to the mainland by a small bridge. The Riva faces the channel that separates Trogir from the island of Ciovo, and has several cafes from which tourists and residents can watch the sailboats traverse the waterway. The city was recently named a UNESCO World Heritage site.

  • Myanmar
