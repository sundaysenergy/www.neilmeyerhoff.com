TWO LOWER CASTE GIRLS

  • India

These two girls were walking through the lower market in Shimla, Himachel Pradesh, India when I stopped them to take their photograph. Shimla was the hill station to which the British Raj decamped every summer to avoid the heat and monsoons of Delhi. The vertical panoramic photograph was taken with a Hasselblad X-pan camera.

Three Girls At Solstice Festival

  • Ecuador

These three girls, in traditional dress, are at the solstice/harvest festival in the tiny village of Azama in Imbabura province. There were lots of girls running around and I would point the camera at some of them, and they would stop to have their photo taken. Here they wanted to be touching each other, and I don't set up their positioning. I want to get close to the subjects so as to create a connection with them. Olympus OMD EM-5, 19mm, 1/160@f4

Young Woman Looking At Me

  • Morocco

Since most Muslims in Morocco do not like to be photographed, I titled the articulated screen of my camera so that I could look down onto the screen and take photos of people without being too obvious. As there was not much light, and not wanting to use a flash which would attract attention, I knew a slow shutter speed would catch movement of the people around me. This woman, however, saw me looking into the screen, but she stayed still during the exposure. Olympus OMD EM-1, ISO 200, 12mm, 1/5@f5.0.

Click image to enlarge.

Beach Along Southwest Coast

  • Sri Lanka

This beach is typical of many beaches along this coast; soft sand, gentle waves, and no tourists. Few tourists came to Sri Lanka during the civil war that ended five years ago. Development will change this scene as new hotels are built. Just up the road from here a Chinese financed 504 room hotel is being built to cater to tourists. Olympus OMS EM-1, 1/250@f16, 150mm ISO 200
