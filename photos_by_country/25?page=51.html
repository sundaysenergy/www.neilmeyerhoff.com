Altar, St. Laurence Cathedral

  • Croatia

The Cathedral of St. Laurence in Trogir was built in the mid 14th-15th centuries in various architectural styles. The church is a very prominent part of the tiny town that sits on an island set just a few yards off the mainland of the central Dalmatian coast in Croatia. Trogir was recently named a UNESCO World Heritage site.
